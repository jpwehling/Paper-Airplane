Locate a piece of "letter" paper

Lay paper on the surface of a flat table, face up, with the short edges toward you and away.
    orientation is "standard postion"

Fold paper in half creasing the fold
    long side touching other long side

Unfold paper and lay in standard postion
    creased line created is "center line"

Fold paper for the most top left corner to the centerline 
    till it is overlayed with the centerline
    crease the created fold
    repeat previous step on the right side

Repeat previous step 2 times

Pick up paper and fold center line along original crease
    Unfold outer most creased lines
    pinch the remain of the papers together toward the center line
        "paper plane" form completed
